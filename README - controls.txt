KEYBOARD / MOUSE INPUTS:

Exit spawn village by going to either North, South, East or West entrance

Left click to move/attack

When in combat - signaled by GUI:
Q = movement
W = melee
E = ranged
R = end turn

Clicking on each individual button also works

Use Enter to go into areas whilst in world map

Enter for final room when infront of chest

CONTROLLER:

Right bumper - enter for game menu, over and won screens

Right bumper - confirm/move in gameplay

Left bumper - enter

XBOX	PLAYSTATION
A	CROSS		Movement
B	CIRCLE		Ranged
X	SQUARE		Melee
Y	TRIANGLE	End turn

A / CROSS control for final room when infront of chest
